About the Artist

The Sound of Brandon Shephard

Zebra Troop is the solo project of Brandon Shephard, a Texas-based multi-instrumentalist whose music blends psychedelic pop, indie soul, vintage R&B warmth, and modern alternative textures into a sound that feels both nostalgic and new.

Troop Nation is a fan-run community dedicated to celebrating the music, sharing the art, and connecting listeners who feel that same pull to the Zebra Troop sound. This is home for the Herd.
